ABSTRACT

The main objective of this project is to identify the people who are using two wheelers and four wheelers in an particular area. The survey is conducted at Settigunta road , SPSR Nellore District. We as a group of three members interacted with the people of the Settigunta road Through this project we have identified the people who are having two wheelers and people who are having four wheelers
